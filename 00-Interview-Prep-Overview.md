## Interview Strategy

### How to Use These Documents

**Before the interview:**
1. Read all 6 documents to understand breadth of coverage
2. Identify your comfort areas and growth areas
3. Practice articulating key themes in your own voice
4. Connect technical background (Kafka, Spring Boot, blockchain) to answers where relevant
5. Develop your personal perspective (not just memorize answers)

**During the interview:**
1. Use these as frameworks, not scripts
2. Adapt to specific questions asked
3. Connect to personal experience (Barclays work, tech background)
4. Show thinking process, not just conclusions
5. Demonstrate systems thinking approach
6. Ask clarifying questions

**Key interview principles reflected:**

- **Specificity over generality**: Answers grounded in data, examples, India context
- **Nuance over black-and-white**: Acknowledge complexity, trade-offs, context-dependence
- **Systems thinking**: Understand interconnections between issues
- **Personal perspective**: Authentic voice, not just textbook answers
- **Pragmatism**: Balance principle with practical reality
- **Technical integration**: Show how tech enables solutions
- **India lens**: Understand India-specific context and challenges
